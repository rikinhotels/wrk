Policies

e-mail Policy – All e-mails are answered on the same day or within a few working hours. Our e-mails are short and crisp and attachments are sent only if required.

Contact Management Policy – All contact details provided by the guest would be used with reference to a particular reservation only.

anti-SPAM Policy – e-mails would be sent pertaining to communications for a particular reservation only. No unsolicited e-mails under a guise of Newsletters, Special offers, deals etc. would be sent. We do not send unnecessary attachments and season’s greetings messages.

Hotel listing Policy – Woods Resort Karnala neither charges the hotel nor is under any sort of contract or obligation from any hotel for listing. Listing and de-listing are at complete discretion of Woods Resort Karnala team. De-listings are done following regular reviews (Site visit and/or review of customer and reservation team feedback/complaints).   

Tariffs and Packages – We offer same Tariff/ packages offered by Hotels and do not levy any service charge for reservations.

Tariffs Updation – We update tariffs on the website regularly however tariffs are purely indicative and may vary for specific dates or periods. We request clients to call our no’s for all offline bookings.

Payments – Any payments received for a reservation is forwarded to the respective hotel/resorts within a couple of days of the receipt. 

Refunds – Any refunds are subject to guidelines laid out by the respective hotel/resort. No service charge are charged for processing cancellations. 

Photographs – Photos displayed on Hotel information pages are clicked/scanned specific to the hotel taken either from hotel brochures or clicked by team Woods Resort Karnala. 

Copyright – Photos displayed on hotellinksindia.com are either taken from hotel brochures or taken by Woods Resort Karnala Team. Copyright Laws apply for photos taken by Woods Resort Karnala Team. 

Legal Liability in Event of unauthorised use of Photographs – Companies / entities found to be using Woods Resort Karnala Photographs without prior permission or authorisation will be liable to the tune of Rs. 15000 per photograph.
